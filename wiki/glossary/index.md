# Glossary

See [terms](terms.md) for the full list. The terms most load-bearing for a
new agent: **Maki** (the vendored VM), **`.wal`** (the Modern skin
container), **windowholder** + **GUID component** (how detached/docked panels
are referenced — the bare-vs-prefixed GUID gotcha), and **gammaset** (Color
Themes).
